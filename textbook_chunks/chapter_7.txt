### Chapter: Fractions

## What is a Fraction?

A fraction represents equal parts of a whole.

Example:
1/2 → half  
1/4 → one-fourth

---

## Fraction as Equal Share

If 1 roti is shared among:
2 people → each gets 1/2  
4 people → each gets 1/4  

More people → smaller share

---

## Unit Fractions

Fractions with numerator 1:
1/2, 1/3, 1/4...

---

## Comparing Fractions

Important idea:
Smaller denominator → larger share (for unit fractions)

Example:
1/2 > 1/4

---

## Fraction as Part of Whole

Whole can be divided in many ways but equal parts are important.

---

## Fractions on Number Line

Fractions can be placed between numbers:
0 --- 1/2 --- 1

---

## Key Terms

Numerator → top number  
Denominator → bottom number  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Fractions represent parts of a whole
- Equal division is important
- Helps measure and compare quantities
