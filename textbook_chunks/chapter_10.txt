### Chapter: Integers

## Beyond Whole Numbers

Numbers extend beyond 0:
- positive numbers (+)
- negative numbers (−)

---

## Number Line

Numbers exist:
… −3, −2, −1, 0, 1, 2, 3 …

Left of 0 → negative  
Right of 0 → positive  

---

## Integers

Integers include:
- positive numbers
- negative numbers
- zero

---

## Real-Life Meaning

Examples:
- floors in a building
- temperature
- money gain/loss

---

## Addition of Integers

Movement idea:
+ → move right/up  
− → move left/down  

---

## Opposites (Inverse)

+3 and −3 cancel each other

(+3) + (−3) = 0

---

## Comparing Integers

- Positive > 0  
- Negative < 0  
- −5 < −3  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Integers include negative numbers
- Number line helps understanding
- Opposites cancel each other
