### Chapter: Playing with Constructions

## Drawing with Compass

A compass is used to:
- draw circles
- measure equal distances

---

## Circle

A circle is formed by all points at equal distance from a centre.

Centre → fixed point  
Radius → distance from centre

---

## Construction Basics

Using ruler and compass:
- draw lines
- draw circles
- create patterns

---

## Squares and Rectangles

Rectangle:
- opposite sides equal
- all angles 90°

Square:
- all sides equal
- all angles 90°

---

## Rotation

Rotating a shape does not change:
- side lengths
- angles

---

## Key Takeaways

- Compass helps create accurate figures
- Geometry depends on properties of shapes
- Shapes remain same even when rotated
