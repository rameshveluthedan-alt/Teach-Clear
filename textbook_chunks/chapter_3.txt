## Supercells

A supercell is a number that is:
- Greater than all adjacent numbers

Used to identify patterns in grids.

---

- Read the same forwards and backwards

## Kaprekar Constant (6174)

Process:
1. Rearrange digits
2. Subtract smallest from largest
3. Repeat

Result:
→ Always reaches 6174 (for 4-digit numbers)

## Collatz Conjecture

Rule:
- Even → divide by 2
- Odd → multiply by 3 and add 1

Observation:
→ Sequence eventually reaches 1
