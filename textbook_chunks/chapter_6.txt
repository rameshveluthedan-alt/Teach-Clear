### Chapter: Perimeter and Area

## Perimeter

Perimeter is the total distance around a shape.

---

## Perimeter of Shapes

Rectangle:
Perimeter = 2 × (length + breadth)

Square:
Perimeter = 4 × side

Triangle:
Perimeter = sum of all sides

---

## Real-Life Use

Perimeter is used in:
- fencing fields
- measuring boundaries
- calculating distance travelled

---

## Example Thinking

If a rectangle has:
length = 3 m, breadth = 2 m

Perimeter = 2 × (3 + 2) = 10 m

---

## Key Takeaways

- Perimeter measures boundary length
- Add all sides to find perimeter
- Used in real-life situations
