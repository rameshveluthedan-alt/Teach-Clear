### Chapter: Symmetry

## What is Symmetry?

Symmetry is when a figure repeats in a balanced way.

---

## Line of Symmetry

A line that divides a figure into two identical halves.

If folded:
→ both halves overlap perfectly

---

## Examples

- Butterfly
- Flowers
- Rangoli

---

## Multiple Lines of Symmetry

Some shapes have more than one symmetry line.

Example:
Square → 4 lines of symmetry

---

## Reflection Symmetry

One half is a mirror image of the other.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Symmetry creates balance
- Folding helps identify symmetry
- Many shapes have multiple symmetries
